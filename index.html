Welcome To Forecast-Forge

Forecast-Forge is your go-to platform whenever and wherever you want to find the weather conditions. Clicking on grant location enables you to know the weather conditions of your current location.
AND just enter the name of the place you want to know the weather condition of, click on the search button, and just within seconds the weather forecast will be on your screen. Along with this, the weather forecast of the next three days will also be displayed. This is what makes Forecast Forge different from other weather apps.
